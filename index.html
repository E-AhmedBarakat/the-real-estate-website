FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you only sell luxury properties?

While we specialize in premium real estate, our portfolio includes excellent investment apartments and family houses across various price points.

Can non-residents buy property in the Middle East?

Yes, in many designated freehold areas. Our legal team will guide you through the specific regulations of the country you are purchasing in.

How long does the buying process take?

Typically, a cash transaction takes 2-4 weeks, while a financed purchase may take 6-8 weeks from signing the initial agreement.
